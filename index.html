Winter Wonderland

Winter Wonderland

Released on the 7/12/2020, Winter Wonderland is a game in active development by the Noodle Tree team, and has had a recent update on the 13/12/2020.

The two main conventions issued by the game jam were "Suprise" and "Festive". The game (I won't spoil) is a First Person Shooter situated outside abandoned santa factory, and is a Battle Royale style game, if you will, has a large map including rocks, wire, and modelled terrain scattered around the playing field.

The most recent update by date of writing, includes but is not limited to:

  • Ranged Enemies
  • Wave counter
  • Adjusted damage, speeds and waves
  • Improved hitboxes
  • Improved weapon accuracy and damage
  • Improved SFX
  • Nerfed Slo-Mo
